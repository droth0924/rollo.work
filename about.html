Dylan Rollo Roth is currently pursuing his Master of City Planning degree at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. He holds a Bachelors of Architecture from Louisiana State University with a minor in Sociology. His research interest encompasses urban science and data literacy as tools to decode the opaque structure of public policy within urban planning. Through the use of storytelling, he hopes to translate the complex spatial relationships within the production of our built environment.
